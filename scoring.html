Scoring System

After submitting your answers, the AI's top answer will become highlighted gold.

The AI's three runner-up answers will become highlighted silver.

And the AI's four weakest answers will remain the same colour.
A gold answer in the gold section will score you the maximum 12 points.

A silver answer in the gold section will score you 5 points.

A gold answer or a silver answer in the silver section will score you 5 points.

Leaving gold answers or silver answers in your Answer Choices box scores you 0 points.

Placing weak answers in the gold section or silver section also scores you 0 points.
If you submit the gold answer in the gold section and all three silver answers in the silver section, you will score 33 points. An 8 point bonus.

Therefore it is possible to score all 99 points within 3 rounds of play. That is how you truly BEAT GPT.
